2. Why the LRAS Curve Might Shift:

    - Capital 
    - Natural Resources 
    - Technology 
3.  Why the SRAS Curve Might Shift

    - Capital

    - People’s expectations of the price level

        - expected price level ⬇️ -> product cost ⬇️ -> SRAS shift to the right
