Supply-side economists improve the workings of free markets using:
1. *Market Orientated Supply-Side Policies* -> ?
2. *Interventionist Supply-Side Policies* -> ?


Warning: 它们不是“曲线决定因素”本身，而是**政府可以做的动作(policy menu)**，目的是去改变[[11 Aggregate Demand and Aggregate Supply#Why the SRAS Curve Might Shift|L, C, NR, T, PE]]（或者改变市场如何把资源配置到这些东西上）。 

---

1. Market Orientated Supply-Side Policies
    - Reform tax and welfare policy
    - Flexible Labour Markets
    - Reducing Government Spending
    - Privatization and Deregulation
2. Interventionist Supply-Side Policies
    - Infrastructure Investment
        - Governments involved where there are long term benefits
    - Investment in Education and Training
    - Research and Development (R&D)
        - Research bodies, tax relief or grants
    - Regional or Industrial Policies
        - Designed to encourage investment and the regional multiplier
