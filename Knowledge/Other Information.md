# Euro
## OCA
an *optimum currency area (OCA)* or *optimal currency region (OCR)* is a geographical region in which it would maximize economic efficiency to have the entire region share a single currency.
- **低通胀国家**：  ✔️ 货币政策好 → ✔️ 放弃代价大 → ❌ 门槛不低    
- **高通胀国家**：  ❌ 政策不可信 → ✔️ 想“搭便车” → ✔️ 门槛反而低
- four main criteria for an OCA:
    - High labor mobility throughout the area.
    - Capital mobility and price and wage flexibility
    - A [currency risk-sharing](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/c/currency-risk-sharing.asp) or fiscal mechanism to share risk across countries in the OCA.
        - The better the fiscal transfer mechanism within a monetary union, the more likely it is that the benefits of a monetary union will outweigh the costs.
    - similar [[9 Business Cycles|business cycles]]
- The net benefits of joining a monetary union are higher for countries that face a higher inflation bias.
- The more integrated the labour markets are within a common currency area, the less need there is for independent monetary policy.

# Bank
 the reasons banking crises cause recessions: **credit crunch**
## De-leveraging
- 杠杆（leverage） = 借钱来投资  
- 去杠杆（de-leveraging） =  减少负债、还债、卖资产、缩表

在现实中，去杠杆通常意味着：
- 银行/金融机构
- 企业
- 投资者
同时想做一件事：“我负债太高了，我要降风险。”

- ✅ De-leveraging pushes down asset prices, undermining banks’ solvency.
- ❌ De-leveraging increases the value of the assets that banks hold on their balance sheets.
