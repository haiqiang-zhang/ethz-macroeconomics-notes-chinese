Physical capital consists of man-made goods that are used to produce other goods and services.
Physical capital 是 [[Factors of Production]] 里的一个成员（capital 这个要素的主要内容）。
# Common examples
Typical examples of physical capital include:
* 🏭 Factories and industrial buildings
* 🛠️ Machinery and equipment
* 🚚 Transportation equipment (trucks, freight vehicles, ships)
* 🖥️ Production-related computers and servers
* 🛣️ Infrastructure (roads, bridges, ports)
* ⚙️ Industrial robots and assembly lines
