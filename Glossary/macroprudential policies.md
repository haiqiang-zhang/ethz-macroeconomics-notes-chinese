Short Desc.: 宏观审慎政策
---
***Macroprudential policies*** are designed to limit risk and enhance stability in the financial sector. 
- ***Deposit insurance systems*** protect savings even if the bank fails. 
- Higher reserve and ***liquidity requirements***.
- Higher ***capital requirements***.
- Central banks act as ***lender of last resort***.
