**Transfer payments are payments made by the government to individuals or groups _without receiving any goods or services in return_.**

They are basically **money transfers**, not purchases.

# Examples of transfer payments

- **Social Security payments** (retirement benefits)
- **Unemployment benefits**
- **Welfare payments**
- **Disability benefits**
- **Scholarships or grants**
- **Subsidies to farmers or industries**
